# Artificial Intelligence

## Models

A compact reference to popular large models and model hubs — their commonly-cited strengths and whether they are open-source or commercial, for quick comparison.

| Model                                     | Strengths                                                                                                                                                                     | Type                                      |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| [ChatGPT](https://openai.com/)            | Strong conversational ability and broad general knowledge; excellent at multi-turn dialogue, drafting, and code generation; large plugin ecosystem for extended capabilities. | Commercial                                |
| [Claude](https://claude.ai/)              | Focus on helpfulness and safety; handles very long context well; strong summarization and creative writing with fewer hallucinations.                                         | Commercial                                |
| [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/)  | Web-aware, citation-backed answers for up-to-date factual queries; fast research and source-tracing for verification.                                                         | Commercial                                |
| [CoPilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com/) | In-editor coding assistant with context-aware completions, multi-language support, and tight GitHub/IDE integration that speeds development.                                  | Commercial                                |
| [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/app)   | Multimodal reasoning and strong problem-solving (math/logic); large-context understanding and deep integration with Google's product ecosystem.                               | Commercial                                |
| [Grok](https://x.com/xai)                 | Fast, personality-driven conversational model with a developer focus; practical tooling for coding and ops workflows.                                                         | Commercial                                |
| [LLaMA 2 / LLaMA 3](https://ai.meta.com)  | Open-weight research models, easy to fine-tune and deploy; popular for on-prem customization and research experiments.                                                        | Open (weights available)                  |
| [Mistral](https://mistral.ai)             | Compact, high-performance open models praised for efficient inference and strong generation quality relative to model size.                                                   | Open                                      |
| [Falcon](https://falconllm.tii.ae)        | Open-source models optimized for inference and low-cost self-hosting; useful for self-hosted deployments.                                                                     | Open                                      |
| [Cohere](https://cohere.ai)               | Enterprise-ready generation and embeddings with strong semantic search and classification support.                                                                            | Commercial                                |
| [AI21 / Jurassic](https://www.ai21.com)   | Commercial models often used for long-form generation and creative writing; alternative to larger cloud offerings.                                                            | Commercial                                |
| [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/)   | Central model hub and platform hosting thousands of open and commercial models, with model cards, community contributions, and deployment/inference tooling.                  | Platform (hosts open & commercial models) |
